  3. To comply with the legal investigation by the judicial department.

  4. It is due to the investigation or use by the competent authority.

  5. The Company believes in good faith that the disclosure of your personal information is required by law.

  8. When you use the Company’s products and services through third- party products and services.

  10. It is required for business transfers and changes in the Company’s organization.

  11. It is required under emergency situations, including situations where it is required to protect the safety of the Company’s employees, customers, or any other person, or to take legal action.
